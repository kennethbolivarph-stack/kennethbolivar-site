About Me

Get to know the person behind the strategies and systems.

I help businesses get found and scale smarter

I'm Kenneth Bolivar — a digital services professional based in the Philippines, working with clients across Australia, the US, and globally. I specialize in the intersection of SEO, AI, and automation.

My journey started with technical SEO — digging into page indexing, caching policies, and performance optimization. Over time, I expanded into AI visibility optimization (AEO and GEO), helping businesses get cited in AI-powered search results, and no-code automation using platforms like Make.com and Zapier.

I'm self-taught and endlessly curious. I spend my days researching emerging trends, testing new tools, and building systems that deliver real results. I believe in transparency, clean communication, and treating every project like my own business depends on it.

My approach

Every engagement begins with a deep audit and research phase. I don't do cookie-cutter solutions. I look at your specific situation — your industry, competitors, tech stack, and goals — and build a strategy from there.

I work lean: minimal overhead, maximum impact. You get enterprise-quality thinking at freelancer speed and pricing. I use AI tools extensively in my workflow — not to cut corners, but to deliver more thorough analysis and faster turnaround.
