About Me

Self-taught builder. Career-switcher. Systems thinker.

I'm not an expert. I just love figuring things out.

I'm Kenneth Bolivar — based in the Philippines, working remotely with clients globally. I build AI-powered automation systems using no-code and low-code tools. That's what I know, and that's what I'm good at.

My background is operations — 5+ years as a delivery partner running high-accountability, metrics-driven work every single day. Route optimization, real-time problem solving, maintaining a 5-star rating with zero excuses. That discipline is what I bring into automation.

I taught myself Make.com, Airtable, Claude, and the OpenAI APIs by building things — cross-platform social media automation, inventory management systems, AI-powered job analysis tools, bidirectional data syncs. I don't learn from courses. I learn by shipping.

How I work

Give me a set of instructions and I'll figure it out. I don't need hand-holding — I need clarity on what you want built, and then I go build it. I research, prototype, test, and deliver.

I'm a career-switcher and I'm transparent about that. I don't have 10 years of automation consulting on my resume. What I have is the ability to take any process, break it down into logic, and turn it into a system that runs without you touching it. That's the value.
