About Me

Career-switcher. AI-powered. Ready to handle the marketing work you don’t have time for.

I handle the marketing work you don’t have time for

I’m Kenneth Bolivar — a career-switcher moving into digital marketing, based in the Philippines and working with clients globally. I focus on helping small teams and solo founders stay consistent, organized, and data-driven without needing a full in-house marketer.

I won’t pretend to be a senior strategist. What I offer is something different: reliable execution, clear communication, and the ability to use AI tools to get essential marketing work done faster and more thoroughly than you could between meetings.

I’m self-taught and endlessly curious. I spend my time learning new tools, testing workflows, and building systems that actually work. I believe in transparency, documented processes, and treating every task like my reputation depends on it — because it does.

How I work

I use AI for first drafts, research, data analysis, and surfacing quick wins — then apply human judgment for quality, tone, and context. You get faster turnaround without sacrificing accuracy.

I’m happy to learn your stack and work within your existing systems. I document everything, respect your SOPs, and communicate clearly in writing. If you’re looking for someone reliable who uses AI to get more done — not to cut corners — I’m your person.
