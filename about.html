About — Kenneth Bolivar

I help founders and lean teams stay organized, responsive, and efficient.

Hi there! I’m Kenneth.

I help founders and small teams reduce manual work through executive support systems, workflow automation, and custom AI assistants.

I come from a background in structured, high-accountability work, and I bring that same discipline into every system I build — clear workflows, practical logic, and support that actually helps day-to-day operations run better.

After years in high-accountability operations, I now combine that real-world discipline with AI to give founders executive-level support — without the cost or complexity of a big team. I don’t just manage tasks; I build calm, reliable systems that handle the details for you.

How I Help

Practical and systems-oriented: I focus on workflows that remove repetitive admin, reduce missed steps, and make daily operations easier to manage.

Built around your workflow: I map how your current process works, identify what can be automated, and build a system that fits your business instead of forcing a complicated setup.

Human + AI partnership: I bring real judgment and care, while the AI handles the repetitive parts. The result is support that feels personal, not robotic.

  • Transitioned my skills into helping founders reclaim their time through simple, effective AI systems
