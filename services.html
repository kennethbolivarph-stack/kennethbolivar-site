Services — Kenneth Bolivar

Three focused ways I help businesses reduce manual work and improve operational flow.

1. Executive Support System

Inbox triage, calendar support, executive briefing, request routing, and organized follow-through built into one practical support system.

  • Smart Inbox — Emails are automatically sorted, prioritized, and replied to in your tone.
  • Calendar & Scheduling — Meetings are booked, rescheduled, and protected without endless back-and-forth.
  • One-Page Briefings — Before any meeting you get a simple summary with context and action items.
  • 24/7 Telegram Front Desk — Clients message anytime. The system answers instantly and only escalates what needs you.
  • Instant Knowledge Access — Ask anything about your business and get answers pulled from your history.

2. AI Workflow Automation

Automate repetitive tasks, approvals, routing, updates, and recurring operational work using tools like Make.com, Airtable, and Notion.

  • Sales follow-ups that send themselves at the right time
  • Order → inventory → supplier alerts that flow without you
  • Team notifications, approvals, and reporting in the background
  • Data that stays clean and synced across all your tools
  • Finished system plus simple instructions for future tweaks

3. Custom AI Assistant

Build AI assistants that can answer questions, retrieve business context, support requests, and help your team act faster using your own knowledge base.

  • Analyzes job applications, proposals, or documents
  • Turns rough ideas into polished content or emails
  • Researches topics and gives ready-to-use summaries
  • Insights and answers based on your own data
  • Trained on your business, not generic ChatGPT

How it works

1

You explain it once
